Kabir Cafe

Kabir Cafe or Neeraj Arya's cafe is a band of Genre Folk-Rock found by NEERAJ ARYA in Mumbai 2013 . Neeraj influenced by his guru Prahlad Singh Tipaniya Ji .He started singing the melodies and dohe of Kabeer Saahb in his own style in his own way he used to perform these melodies on street of Mumbai there he met Mukund Ramaswamy (violinst of Kabir Cafe) after some time more peoples joined them and created a band and represnted as a Kabir Cafe .

How the band became popular?

After the Series Scam 1992 based on the story of Harshad Mehta this band became very popular. There is a song "Mat Kar Maya Ko Ahankaar" the song which tell us about the meaning of life . "Dont be deluded by illusion".Dont take pride in body Its as frgile as clay"

Reason behind the name Kabir Cafe?

Kabir was a Poet musician and a saint he composed his verses with simple hindi words and there's always a very deep meaning behind every single word.Kabeer cafe used these meaningful dohe and created a diffrent version of it and this is what makes them very different from other bands.
